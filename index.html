A little about me

I'm a young adult who recently graduated high school in the year of 2013. I am part of Cherry Creek Transitions, which helps young adults get jobs, move out and live on their own, and learn life skills to help them throughout the rest of their lives.

I also attend SeedPaths which is an 8 week professional and web development boot camp, teaching young adults the fundamental and necessary skills to gain entry into the IT industry. I have a huge passion for computers and technology, having grown up around technology. In my time with SeedPaths I have learned how to become more professional, keep high energy, be confident, manage myself, and be intellectually curious as well as learn various coding languages such as HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, and SQL.
